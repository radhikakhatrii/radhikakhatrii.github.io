Assistive X for Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)

    • Assistive X for ALS is Pakistan’s first Urdu-based eye-controlled device developed to empower non-verbal ALS patients by enabling them to communicate effectively.
    • Designed with user-friendly, culturally relevant features, the device uses eye-tracking technology and visual interfaces to meet the unique communication needs of Urdu-speaking individuals.
    • This project addresses a critical gap in assistive technology, allowing patients to express their needs, connect with loved ones, and improve their quality of life despite mobility challenges.
